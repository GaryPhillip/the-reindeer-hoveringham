Settle in

Set beside the crack of leather on willow in Hoveringham, The Reindeer is a village pub on the cricket ground. Head Chef Billy sources from Dunwood Farms and Packington Farms, and creates the menu when the produce dictates. Open Wednesday to Sunday. Come and eat with us.

What we cook

Billy creates the menu around the best seasonal ingredients he can find. It could be dry-aged beef from Dunwood Farms or corn-fed chicken from Packington. These are the farms he trusts, and they dictate what goes on the plate. No fixed dishes. No standing menu. Just what’s exceptional that week, cooked with restraint and care.

Lunch & dinner, Wednesday to Saturday · Set lunch from £27 · Sundays from £33

Billy Mantle

From the kitchen

The Reindeer exists for one reason: to serve the very best produce we can get our hands on. Great ingredients have a story behind them: the people who raise them, grow them and care for them. Our responsibility is to honour that work by cooking with skill, restraint and attention to detail.

The menu changes with the seasons. The ingredients lead the way. And every decision, from the kitchen to the dining room, is guided by a commitment to quality.

Simple in principle. Uncompromising in execution.

— Billy Mantle

Every Sunday, 12 to 6

Sunday lunch

Four options on the table every Sunday: dry-aged sirloin from Dunwood Farms, roast pork belly, corn-fed chicken from Packington, and a wild mushroom pithivier for those who’d rather not. All served with roast potatoes, seasonal vegetables, Yorkshire pudding and gravy.

2 courses £33 · 3 courses £39

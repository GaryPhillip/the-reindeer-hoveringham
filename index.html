Settle In

Sit, relax and hear the crack of leather on willow in Hoveringham. The Reindeer is a village pub sited next to the picturesque cricket ground. Head Chef and Owner Billy works closely with trusted local suppliers to source the very best ingredients and then creates the menu to let the produce shine. Open Wednesday to Sunday. Come and eat with us.

What We Cook

Our chefs build each menu around the finest seasonal ingredients available that week. From dry-aged beef reared at Dunwood Farms to fresh seafood from Flying Fish Cornwall, every ingredient is sourced from producers we know and trust. The seasons and the quality of the produce dictate what appears on the plate.

There are no signature dishes and no standing menu, just exceptional ingredients, thoughtfully prepared and cooked with restraint, care and respect.

Billy Mantle

The Kitchen

The Reindeer exists for one reason: to serve the very best produce we can get our hands on. Great ingredients have a story behind them: the people who raise them, grow them and care for them. Our responsibility is to honour that work by cooking with skill, restraint and attention to detail.

The menu changes with the seasons and the ingredients lead the way. It’s also shaped by how I enjoy eating myself: gathering around a table, ordering a variety of dishes and sharing great food with family and friends. Many of our dishes are designed with that in mind, allowing guests to experience more of the menu together. Equally, if you’d rather enjoy a dish all to yourself, that’s absolutely fine too.

Every decision, from the kitchen to the dining room, is guided by a commitment to quality.

Simple in principle. Uncompromising in execution.

— Billy Mantle

On The Terrace · Thursday to Sunday

Bold flavours.
Open skies.

Permanently situated on The Reindeer’s terrace, SHIO is our Asian-inspired street food kitchen, bringing bold flavours and relaxed outdoor dining to Hoveringham. Cooked over traditional Japanese charcoal, the menu draws inspiration from across Asia.

Popular dishes include Korean fried chicken with our signature SHIO sauce, teriyaki beef skewers from the grill and a regularly changing selection of seasonal specials. Whether you’re joining us for a quick bite, drinks with friends or a relaxed meal in the sun, SHIO offers a more casual dining experience alongside The Reindeer’s restaurant.
